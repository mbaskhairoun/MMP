Transcript Requests

Request official transcripts from the University of Toronto for credentialing, licensing, or further education.

Official transcripts are required for many professional purposes, including hospital credentialing, medical licensing applications, fellowship programs, and immigration processes. The University of Toronto Office of the Registrar manages all transcript requests for current students and alumni.

Transcripts are available as official printed documents sent by mail or as official electronic transcripts delivered securely to the recipient institution.

How to Request a Transcript

Step 1: Verify Your Identity

Log in to ACORN (Accessible Campus Online Resource Network) using your UTORid. If you no longer have access, contact the IT Help Desk to reactivate your account.

Step 2: Submit Your Request

Navigate to the Transcript Request section in ACORN. Select the type of transcript (printed or electronic), the number of copies, and the delivery address.

Step 3: Pay the Fee

Complete payment online using a credit card. Fees are charged per copy. You will receive a confirmation email once payment is processed.

Step 4: Track Your Order

Monitor the status of your transcript request through ACORN. You will receive email notifications when your transcript has been sent.

Fees and Processing Times

Service Fee (per copy) Processing Time
Official Printed Transcript (mail within Canada) $12.00 CAD 5–7 business days
Official Printed Transcript (international mail) $18.00 CAD 10–15 business days
Official Electronic Transcript $15.00 CAD 1–3 business days
Rush Processing (add-on) $25.00 CAD 1 business day

All fees are in Canadian dollars. Prices are subject to change. Please check ACORN for the most current fee schedule.
