Alumni & Student Mentorship Mixer

Connect, mentor, and build the next generation of medical leaders.

Networking

About This Event

Connect with current Temerty Medicine students seeking career guidance, research advice, and professional mentorship. This casual afternoon event pairs alumni with students for structured speed-mentoring rounds, followed by open networking.

Whether you graduated five years ago or thirty, your experience and insights are invaluable to students navigating the early stages of their medical careers. The Mentorship Mixer is designed to create meaningful, one-on-one connections in a relaxed and welcoming environment at one of the University of Toronto's most historic venues.

What to Expect

  • Speed mentoring rounds (15 min each)
  • Open networking session
  • Panel: "Advice I Wish I Had as a Student"
  • Light refreshments and snacks
  • Mentorship program sign-up opportunity

Who Should Attend

This event is open to all Temerty Medicine alumni and current students. Whether you are looking to give back as a mentor, seeking guidance as a student, or simply want to expand your professional network, we welcome you.

Alumni from all specialties and career stages are encouraged to participate. No prior mentoring experience is needed — just a willingness to share your journey.
