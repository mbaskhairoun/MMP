Career Services

Supporting Temerty Medicine alumni at every stage of their professional journey.

The Career Services Office provides comprehensive support to Temerty Faculty of Medicine alumni, whether you are a recent graduate exploring practice options or a seasoned physician considering a career transition. Our team of dedicated career advisors understands the unique landscape of medical careers in Canada and internationally.

All services are available to registered alumni at no additional cost. Book an appointment through the Alumni Portal or contact our office directly.

Our Services

Career Counselling

One-on-one sessions with experienced career advisors who specialize in medical career pathways, leadership transitions, and work-life balance strategies.

Job Board

Access exclusive job postings from hospitals, clinics, research institutions, and health organizations that actively recruit UofT medical graduates.

Resume Review

Have your CV or resume reviewed by professionals who understand what hiring committees and academic institutions are looking for in medical candidates.

Interview Preparation

Practice with mock interviews tailored to medical positions, including academic appointments, hospital leadership roles, and research positions.
