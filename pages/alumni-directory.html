Alumni Directory

Find and reconnect with over 35,000 Temerty Faculty of Medicine graduates worldwide.

The Temerty Medicine Alumni Directory is your gateway to a global network of physicians, researchers, and health leaders. Whether you are looking to reconnect with former classmates, find a specialist colleague, or expand your professional network, our directory puts the entire alumni community at your fingertips.

All registered alumni can search the directory after logging into the Alumni Portal. Your privacy is important to us — you control what information is visible to other alumni through your profile settings.

Search the Directory

Multiple ways to find the alumni you are looking for.

Search by Name

Look up alumni by first name, last name, or maiden name to reconnect with classmates and colleagues.

Search by Class Year

Find everyone from your graduating class or browse alumni from any year since 1843.

Search by Specialty

Filter by medical specialty to connect with alumni in your field or explore other disciplines.

Search by Location

Discover alumni in your city, province, or country — perfect for building local connections abroad.

A Truly Global Network
