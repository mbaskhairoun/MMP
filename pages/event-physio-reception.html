UofT Physiotherapy Alumni Reception

A formal afternoon of networking, connection, and celebration at historic Knox College.

Reception

About This Event

Dear Alumni,

You are warmly invited to attend a U of T Physiotherapy Alumni Reception taking place at the historic Knox College. This formal afternoon reception will bring together UofT physiotherapy alumni, students, and professionals for networking, conversation, and connection within the physiotherapy community.

What to Expect

  • Networking with physiotherapy professionals and clinic representatives
  • Opportunities to connect with fellow alumni and current students
  • Light program remarks
  • Light snacks and refreshments

Sponsors & Partners

This event is made possible through the generous support of leading physiotherapy clinics and healthcare institutions across Toronto. See our full list of sponsors below.
