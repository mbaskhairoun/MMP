Class of 2016 — 10-Year Reunion

Celebrate a decade since graduation with classmates, faculty, and friends.

Reunion

About This Event

Celebrate a decade since graduation. Join classmates, faculty, and friends for an evening of memories, dinner, and reconnection. Formal dinner program, keynote address by a Class of 2016 alumnus, photo retrospective, and live music.

This milestone reunion brings together graduates from the Temerty Faculty of Medicine's Class of 2016 for a memorable evening celebrating ten years of achievement, growth, and contribution to the medical community. Whether you have stayed in Toronto or are joining us from across the globe, this is your night to reconnect and reminisce.

What to Expect

  • Cocktail reception and registration
  • Formal sit-down dinner
  • Keynote address from featured alumnus
  • Class photo retrospective slideshow
  • Live entertainment and dancing
  • Commemorative gift for attendees

Additional Details

Dress Code: Business formal

Cost: Complimentary for Class of 2016 alumni; $75 for guests

Space is limited. We encourage all Class of 2016 alumni to register early to secure their spot. Guest tickets can be purchased during the registration process.
