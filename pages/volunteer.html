Volunteer Opportunities

Give back to the Temerty Medicine community and make a lasting impact on the next generation.

Our alumni volunteers are the backbone of the Temerty Faculty of Medicine community. Whether you have a few hours a year or want to take on a leadership role, there are many meaningful ways to contribute your time, expertise, and passion to support current students and fellow alumni.

Volunteering strengthens your connection to the university, builds your professional network, and helps shape the future of medical education in Canada.

Ways to Volunteer

Mentoring

Guide medical students and residents through our formal Mentorship Program. Share your clinical wisdom, career advice, and life lessons with the next generation.

Events

Help organize and run alumni events, from annual reunions and galas to local chapter meetups and professional development workshops.

Committees

Join an alumni association committee focused on governance, awards, diversity and inclusion, or strategic planning. Help shape the direction of our community.

Fundraising

Support fundraising campaigns for student scholarships, research initiatives, and faculty programs. Serve as a campaign ambassador within your network.

Guest Lectures

Share your expertise by delivering guest lectures, clinical case presentations, or career panels for current medical students and residents.
